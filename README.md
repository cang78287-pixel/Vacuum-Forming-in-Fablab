# Vacuum-Forming-in-Fablab

For the group project of "Just a Group", in the Fablab scenario, we upgraded the material system and core technologies of the vacuum forming process. By adopting bioplastics and vacuum bag molding technology, we achieved the greening and substitutability upgrading of the process.
## 真空成型工艺生物塑料与技术改造项目描述
## Project Description: Modification of Vacuum Forming Process with Bioplastic in Fablab
传统真空成型工艺依赖热塑性塑料片材，存在材料回收难度大、环境负担重等问题；同时，常规抽真空装置在操作灵活性上也难以完全满足Fablab的探索。基于此，我们团队在Fablab场景中，针对真空成型工艺的材料体系与核心技术进行改造，采用生物塑料和真空袋成型技术，实现了工艺的绿色化与可替代性升级。

The traditional vacuum forming process relies on thermoplastic sheets, which pose problems such as high material recycling difficulty and heavy environmental burden. Meanwhile, conventional vacuum extraction devices are also insufficient in operational flexibility to fully meet the exploration needs of Fablabs. Based on this, our team has modified the material system and core technology of the vacuum forming process in the Fablab scenario, adopting bioplastic and vacuum bagging technology to achieve an environmentally friendly and alternative upgrade of the process.

### 二、项目实施过程与关键成果
### II. Project Implementation Process and Key Achievements

#### 项目实施分为三个阶段：
#### The project implementation is divided into three phases:

#### 第一阶段为生物塑料制作，以100g玉米淀粉、10ml白醋、10g甘油、100ml蒸馏水为原材料制作的可降解生物塑料，并用布料作为载体。

#### Phase 1: Bioplastic Production

Degradable bioplastic are produced using 100g corn starch, 10ml white vinegar, 10g glycerin, and 100ml distilled water, with fabric used as a carrier.

具体步骤：Specific Steps:

1. 准备与混合
   
   · 在托盘或盘子上铺好油纸和布料，备用。
   
   · 将 15克玉米淀粉、100毫升蒸馏水、5-15克甘油 和 10毫升白醋 全部倒入锅中。
   
   · 用刮刀充分搅拌，直到没有大的干淀粉结块，形成均匀的乳白色悬浊液。
   
   Preparation and Mixing
   - Line a tray or plate with oil paper and fabric, set aside for later use.
   - Pour 15g corn starch, 100ml distilled water, 5-15g glycerin, and 10ml white vinegar into a pot.
   - Stir thoroughly with a spatula until there are no large dry starch lumps, forming a uniform milky white suspension.
![material](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/53020735-c96e-4dba-ad8b-859e99109387)
![mix material(1)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ce791e08-178c-48df-a66a-d13ad2a45828)


2. 加热与糊化（关键步骤）
   
   · 将锅置于中火上，开始持续地、缓慢地搅拌。
   
   · 最初液体很稀，随着温度升高，会逐渐变稠并开始冒泡。此时可以转为中小火。
   
   · 继续搅拌，你会发现混合物从乳白色慢慢变得半透明甚至完全透明，质地非常粘稠，像浆糊一样。这个过程大约需要 3-5分钟。
   
   · 重要提示：当混合物成为一团粘稠、光滑、透明的凝胶状物，并且锅底能短暂看见锅底时（即“出焦”现象），就表示糊化完成，可以离火了。
   
   Heating and Gelatinization (Key Step)
   - Place the pot over medium heat and start stirring continuously and slowly.
   - Initially, the liquid is very thin; as the temperature rises, it will gradually thicken and start to bubble. At this point, you can switch to medium-low heat.
   - Continue stirring, and you will notice that the mixture gradually changes from milky white to translucent or even completely transparent, with a very thick texture like paste. This process takes approximately 3-5 minutes.
   - Important note: When the mixture becomes a thick, smooth, and transparent gel, and the bottom of the pot can be briefly seen (i.e., the "scorching" phenomenon), it indicates that gelatinization is complete, and you can remove the pot from the heat.
![heat(1)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/98375ee8-1051-4391-80bf-32abe4cbb765)


3. 混合与整形
   
   · 将布料放入锅中，和煮好的粘稠物均匀混合，动作要快，因为它冷却会很快凝固。
   
   · 将布料取出，在托盘的油纸上平整铺开。
   
   Mixing and Shaping
   - Put the fabric into the pot and mix it evenly with the cooked thick mixture. Act quickly, as the mixture will solidify soon after cooling.
   - Take out the fabric and spread it flat on the oil paper in the tray.
![mix bioplistic and cloth(1)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4e9744de-25d3-4cc2-ab03-363b4c5d4e5c)
![flat the cloth(1)](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/31f00d23-3e0e-4f0e-991f-ef0947c58b43)


4. 减少水分
   
   · 在布料上敷上吸水纸，减少水分，便于后续成型。
   
   Moisture Reduction
   - Cover the fabric with absorbent paper to reduce moisture, facilitating subsequent forming.

#### 第二阶段为装置改造与集成，用热熔胶将模具和盒子结合到一起，盒子给模具提供支撑作用。

#### Phase 2: Device Modification and Integration

The mold and the box are bonded together using hot melt adhesive, and the box provides support for the mold.

#### 第三阶段为抽真空与干燥成型，将在第一阶段制作好的混合了生物塑料的布料覆在模具上，然后把模具和盒子的结合体放到真空袋里，然后用真空泵对真空袋抽真空，待布料和生物塑料的混合物完全干燥变硬，则可得到一个贴合模具形状的物品。

#### Phase 3: Vacuum Extraction and Drying Forming：Cover the mold with the fabric mixed with biomaterials produced in Phase 1, then place the assembly of the mold and the box into a vacuum bag. Use a vacuum pump to extract air from the vacuum bag. When the mixture of the fabric and biomaterials is completely dried and hardened, a product that fits the shape of the mold can be obtained.

完全干燥至少需要2-3天甚至更久（取决于厚度和环境湿度），千万不要在未干透时试图将其撕下，否则会变形或破裂。也可以在材料大致在模具上定型的时候将整个装置从真空袋中取出，然后用烘干机进行干燥。

Complete drying takes at least 2-3 days or even longer (depending on the thickness and environmental humidity). Never attempt to tear it off before it is fully dried, otherwise it will deform or break. Alternatively, when the material is roughly shaped on the mold, the entire device can be taken out of the vacuum bag and then dried using a dryer.

### 三、反馈与收获
### III.  Feedback and Takeaways

在makerfaire中，一些参观者对我们的模型的作用和工作方式产生了困惑，主要原因大致有两点。

其一，模型的可交互性较弱，所以参观者难以理解它的作用，要改进这一点，在条件允许的情况下，或许我们可以准备更多的小模型和材料，组织一个小小的工作坊，让参观者尝试上手制作生物塑料，然后通过我们的简易装置制作模型，通过这样的流程，或许参观者就可以更好地理解我们的装置。

其二，展示的内容不够明确，解释和引导都不够清晰。针对于这一点，首先我们应该将对应的介绍的海报放在装置旁边，让参观者能够直接对应，然后查看海报上的详细信息。其次，我们可以制作更多的生物塑料片材以及成品模型，让参观者可以直接地接触了解这种材料的特性和我们的装置最后能产生的成品，更加直观地了解我们的原理。

At MakerFaire, some visitors were puzzled by how our model functioned and operated, primarily due to two key factors.

Firstly, the model's limited interactivity makes it challenging for visitors to grasp its functionality. To address this, we could organize a hands-on workshop when resources permit. Participants would first try creating bio-plastic prototypes using simple tools, then build models with our device. Through this interactive process, visitors could gain a clearer understanding of our innovation.

Secondly, the displayed content lacks clarity, with insufficient explanations and guidance. To address this, we should first place corresponding promotional posters next to the installation, allowing visitors to directly reference them and access detailed information. Additionally, we could produce more bioplastic sheets and finished product models, enabling visitors to physically interact with the material's characteristics and the final products our installation will create. This hands-on approach would provide a more intuitive understanding of our design principles.

### 四、未来计划与协作邀请
### IV. Future Plans and Collaboration Invitations

本项目通过材料与技术的协同改造，拓展了真空成型工艺在绿色制造领域的应用边界，也为Fablab的开源制造生态注入了环保理念。从技术层面看，实现了生物材料与真空袋成型技术的跨界融合，为类似工艺改造提供了可复制的技术路径；从应用层面看，改造后的工艺可直接服务于创客教育、可持续设计、小型文创产品开发等场景，助力“零废弃”制造目标的实现。

Through the collaborative modification of materials and technology, this project has expanded the application boundary of the vacuum forming process in the field of green manufacturing and injected the concept of environmental protection into the open-source manufacturing ecosystem of Fablabs. From a technical perspective, it has realized the cross-border integration of biomaterials and vacuum bagging technology, providing a replicable technical path for similar process modifications. From an application perspective, the modified process can directly serve scenarios such as maker education, sustainable design, and small-scale cultural and creative product development, contributing to the achievement of the "zero-waste" manufacturing goal.

#### 需要解决的问题：

（1）重点突破生物塑料的耐用性与环境适应性，通过配方调整提升材料的抗冲击强度与耐温范围，目标是使成型品能适应日常使用场景。

（2）探索自动化控温与负压调节系统，缩短单件产品的干燥成型周期。

#### Problems to be Solved:
1. Focus on breaking through the durability and environmental adaptability of biomaterials, and improve the impact strength and temperature resistance range of the materials through formula adjustment, with the goal of enabling the molded products to adapt to daily use scenarios.
2. Explore the automatic temperature control and negative pressure regulation system to shorten the drying and forming cycle of a single product.

#### 本项目改造后的真空成型工艺将全部开源共享。若其他团队或个人希望基于此成果继续探索，可重点关注以下方向：

给材料 / 化学领域人士的建议：可围绕生物塑料的 “实用性升级” 展开，比如测试不同天然添加剂（如竹纤维、甲壳素）对材料耐用性的提升效果，或针对特定场景（如食品接触、户外使用）做专项安全性检测，填补当前生物塑料成型品在实际应用中的性能空白。

给自动化 / 机械领域人士的建议：可尝试将现有工艺与简易自动化结合，比如开发低成本的温控 - 负压联动模块，解决当前工艺 “单次成型效率低”“干燥周期长” 的问题，让技术更适配小批量生产场景。

#### The modified vacuum forming process of this project will be fully open-sourced and shared. If other teams or individuals wish to continue exploring based on this achievement, they can focus on the following directions:
Suggestions for Professionals in Materials/Chemistry Fields
You can focus on the "practical upgrade" of bioplastic. For example, test the effect of different natural additives (such as bamboo fiber, chitin) on improving the durability of materials, or conduct special safety tests for specific scenarios (such as food contact, outdoor use) to fill the performance gaps of current bioplastic molded products in practical applications.

Suggestions for Professionals in Automation/Mechanical Fields
You can try to combine the existing process with simple automation. For example, develop low-cost temperature control-negative pressure linkage modules to solve the problems of "low single-piece forming efficiency" and "long drying cycle" in the current process, making the technology more suitable for small-batch production scenarios.
